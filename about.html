Our Story

Passion for flavor, frozen into every stick.

Frozen With Love

Popsicle Stories began with a simple idea — that a popsicle can be more than a snack; it can be a little moment of joy. We opened our first store in Thiruvallur to share handcrafted frozen treats made from real fruit and honest ingredients.

Every pop is made fresh in small batches — from our now-famous Kunafa Popsicle to comforting Malai Kulfi. No artificial colors, no shortcuts, just flavors we would happily serve our own family.

What started as one counter has grown into a much-loved neighborhood brand, and this is only the beginning of our sweet, frozen story.

Our Mission

To craft joyful frozen treats using real fruit and premium ingredients — handmade in small batches, with a relentless commitment to freshness, hygiene, and a smile with every stick.

Our Vision

To become the most-loved dessert brand in the region — where every popsicle feels like a celebration, every customer feels like family, and every neighborhood has a Popsicle Stories nearby.
